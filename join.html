The Process

How to Join Us

Simply check the BizDogs Living-Learning box on your UMD housing application! There is no additional cost to be a member, but admission is first-come, first-served, so get your application in as soon as possible!

Member Benefits

Why You Should Join Our Team

  • So you can succeed! Students who participate in learning communities have higher GPAs.
  • BizDog students have a higher retention rate than other LSBE students.
  • You can move into your residence hall before other UMD students.
  • Interact with LSBE staff who want you to succeed!
  • Participate in BizDog-only events to advance your educational development and create professional goals!
  • Network with LSBE alumni to gain insight into career paths and success strategies.
  • Attend study groups on your own floor within your BizDog community.
  • Meet a diverse group of students who offer different backgrounds, thoughts, and ideas.
  • Develop long-lasting relationships with your peers!
  • Live among students with similar academic interests.
  • You get guaranteed seats in required BizDog classes.
