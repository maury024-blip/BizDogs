What is BizDogs?

BizDogs is a Living-Learning Community is a group of first-year, pre-business students in the Labovitz School of Business & Economics at the University of Minnesota, Duluth. Members live together in a residence hall community in Lake Superior Hall and take classes together to create a sense of community and thrive both socially & academically.
